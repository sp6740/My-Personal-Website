Taylor Swift: An Icon

Taylor Swift is one of the most iconic pop stars and celebrities in the world. With her start as a country singer and releasing her first album in 2006, she has grown into so many different genres of music and reached so many different types of people. Many have questioned what has caused her to rise to so much fame and why fans are so attracted to the music that she writes. As her last tour, The Eras Tour, has been one of the most successful tours in history, it is interesting to look at the data behind her albums and performances.
